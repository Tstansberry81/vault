# Senior Class Representative — Campaign Speech (May 2025)

Hi, my name is Traveler Stansberry. I have gone to St. Paul's for the past 12 years of my life. I have enjoyed growing up with and getting to know many people here. But the thing I love most about this school is the community built around it. The community to me is so special because everyone genuinely cares about each other and wants the best for one another. I have participated in various parts of it; from being involved in sports such as soccer, squash, and lacrosse; to being a peer tutor and Bridges afterschool mentor; to being the future president of the investment club, and much more.

I think I would be a good Senior Representative because I am willing to listen to new ideas, and I also have the passion about this school and community to get things done. I am running for class representative because I want our final year here to be the best one yet.

This year, student life, specifically student sections at games or events, seemed to have gotten way better than they were last year. I think this is a crucial turning point for our school and our class, as we can finally take ownership and initiative of supporting our classmates in whatever they're doing. We, as the senior class, should make sure we're encouraging student support at all events, and I want to lead that initiative. This will make our final year here at this special place more memorable.

I also want to take charge of creating new events and working with the Spirit Council in facilitating classic traditions, such as the Chili cookoff, or sock hop. These events are what takes our community from being good to being great, and the more we can do to make them better and have more people come, the better our community will be.

This role as class representative also includes being a part of the disciplinary council, and I will, if elected, do my part to make sure all individuals get proper and fair treatment.

The thing that makes St. Paul's special is the people, and for our final year we should try to do everything we can to engage with this great community, and I want to make that happen. So, vote for me. Thanks.
