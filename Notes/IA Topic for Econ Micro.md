# IA Topic for Econ Micro


My topic for the SL Economics Microeconomics IA is change, and how it will affect a specific company in the gaming industry. My question is: How will the changes from Grand Theft Auto 5 to Grand Theft Auto 6 affect the total revenue Rockstar Games generates in 2025. I picked this topic because GTA 6 has been a long awaited video game, following the success of its predecessor GTA 5. There have been many speculations on the future popularity of the game, and I hope to dive deep and figure out how big this game is and how the public will receive it.
