# Gatsby Essay

"The Flaws of Man" — analytical essay on [[The Great Gatsby]].

## Summary
Reads **Tom Buchanan and George Wilson** as twin embodiments of early-20th-century male superiority — Tom through wealth, power, and racism (*The Rise of the Colored Empires*); Wilson through emotional control of Myrtle. Nick (reserves judgment) and Gatsby ("worth the whole damn bunch") are the "good by nurture" exceptions. Thesis: "wealth, power, and emotion corrupts all."
