# Letter to Dad (Dec 2022)

A birthday note written to his father (age ~14). One of the warmest documents in the corpus, and the early baseline for the [[Family and Personal Life|father thread]].

## Summary
Affectionate and grateful — "how happy i am that you are my dad… makes my life whole," noting "a mutual understanding of a lot of things about life." He acknowledges the changes underway (a new sibling, the remarriage, a new business) and pledges constancy: "through all these things, i will still love you, and always will." Closes, half-joking, half-pleading: "ps: stop drinking dumbass."

## Why it matters
The tender starting point against which the later [[Note on His Father (July 2025)]] reads as a real decline. Together they trace the relationship's arc.
