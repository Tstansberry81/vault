# Explicative of Garden of Eden

Close-reading explication of Tracy K. Smith's [[Garden of Eden (poem)]].

## Summary
Thesis: "in contemporary America, consumerism is used as a tool to fill the void in identity created by emotional distress" (here, aging). Reads the grocery store "Garden of Eden" as ironic false paradise, the exotic fruit as luxurious imagery, and "innocence and privacy" as casualties of social media. Strong **structural** point: enjambment pauses only on the consumerist list, so "the only time the narrator feels structure or fullness… is when she is engaging in consumerist culture."
