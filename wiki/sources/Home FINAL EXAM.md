# Home FINAL EXAM

Final-exam essay on [[Home (novel)|Toni Morrison's Home]].

## Summary
Defines **home** as "more than a place; rather, a feeling of familiarity, comfort, peace, and calm." Frank Money's journey to Lotus is spiritual rebirth (the Lotus flower = rebirth/cleansing) from PTSD and alcoholism; Cee's is physical healing from a eugenicist doctor's abuse, culminating in self-reliance. "Returning home is more than an idea: it's a way of completely resetting one's ideas, habits, problems, or beliefs."
