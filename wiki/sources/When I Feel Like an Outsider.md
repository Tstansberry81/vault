# When I Feel Like an Outsider

Personal reflection ([[English Curriculum 2022-2026|English 9]]). One of the most self-revealing sources in the corpus.

## Summary
Traveler describes **social anxiety** at parties — "concealing myself in this veil of falsity, trying to be someone I'm not." Ten years at [[St. Paul's School]] with the same people leaves him grounded but isolated, bad at small talk ("when I try to be authentic, it always comes out as cringe"). The redemptive turn: **writing as his authentic voice** — "instead of talking to people, I would write to them; it helped me better articulate my thoughts in a way that wasn't fake or scripted."

## Why it matters
The clearest statement of why this whole wiki exists: he thinks and connects best through writing. Feeds [[Traveler Stansberry]] and [[Art as Survival]].
