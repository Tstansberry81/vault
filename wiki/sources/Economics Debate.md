# Economics Debate

11th-grade SL Economics debate prep: *"Which economic policy is most effective in promoting long-term growth?"* — Traveler argued the **supply-side** team.

## Summary
A thorough brief: opening speech + pros/cons/rebuttals for all four policy stances (supply-side, demand-side, fiscal, monetary). His supply-side case marshals Germany's Hartz reforms, Ireland's 12.5% corporate tax, South Korea's R&D, and a Reagan (3.6%) vs. Obama (2.3%) GDP comparison, with sourced rebuttals to the inequality, deficit, and market-failure critiques. Uses the same case studies as his [[Economics IA|Econ IAs]] (COVID inflation, Argentina's debt, Japan's lost decades).

## Why it matters
Primary evidence for his [[Political and Economic Views|free-market / supply-side worldview]] — corroborated by an independent note stating the same.
