# Physics IA

HL Physics Internal Assessment: *"How does changing the angle of a ramp affect the friction on a cart?"* (~2,337 words).

## Summary
Vernier motion-sensor + Logger Pro experiment, 16 trials, friction derived from a free-body model (F_f = Ma − Mg sinθ). Carries **uncertainty propagation** (angle ±0.5°, mass) through to the force values. Crucially, his data contradicted theory (friction appearing to *increase* with angle; negative values at 6°/9°), and he **honestly diagnoses the failure** — high uncertainty, the small-angle regime where friction ∝ cos θ barely varies — rather than massaging results.

## Why it matters
The most quant-relevant artifact in the corpus: real experimental rigor + intellectual honesty about error. Weighed in [[UVA and the Quant Question]].
