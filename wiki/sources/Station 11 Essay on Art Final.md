# Station 11 Essay on Art Final

"Survival's Expression" — analytical essay on art in [[Station Eleven]]. (An earlier draft, "Station 11 Essay on Art," also exists in the corpus.)

## Summary
Art is "the language of human emotion" and a basic need that "fills the void." Traces it through the Traveling Symphony's motto "because survival is insufficient," the *Station Eleven* comics (which link Kirsten and Tyler across time), and the Museum of Civilization. Also reads Tyler/the Prophet's [[Faith vs. Knowledge|religious fanaticism]] as exploitation. Thesis: art shapes how people "act, think, and behave."

## Significance
The fullest statement of [[Art as Survival]].
