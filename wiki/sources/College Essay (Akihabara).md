# College Essay (Akihabara)

Traveler's **submitted Common App personal statement** (from the [[Common App (UVA ED 2026)]]).

## Summary
On his [[IB Japanese (SL)|Japan exchange]], he leads his whole group from school in Saitama to **Akihabara** — his first solo navigation of Tokyo's complex train lines (palms sweating). After hours exploring, the group gets lost ~2 miles from the station; because he's the strongest Japanese speaker, **he's the only one who can navigate them back**. He frames the trip as turning "despair and disorientation into belonging and confidence," with the lesson: "the best experiences happen when I challenge myself and get outside my comfort zone."
