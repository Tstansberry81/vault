# Maus Essay

"Showing, not Telling" — analytical essay on [[Maus]].

## Summary
Argues Art Spiegelman makes deliberate **ethical choices about what to depict**: showing the graves and burning bodies for historical accuracy, but censoring a child's death (no face shown) out of empathy for the dead and care for the reader. Art as a balance of "raw truth" and respect — neither distorting the Holocaust nor exploiting its violence.
