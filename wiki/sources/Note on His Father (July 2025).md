# Note on His Father (July 2025)

A short, raw note written at a low point. Recorded here plainly and respectfully because it is central to understanding him; not analyzed beyond his own words.

## Summary
Written while "the most depressed i've ever been," the note expresses feeling unable to get distance from his father and a wish to talk to his mom. Its hardest line draws a distinction he keeps returning to: **"i don't have a dad, i have a father, and there's no way around it."**

## Context within the wiki
Sits at the far end of the arc that begins with the affectionate [[Letter to Dad (Dec 2022)]]. Pairs with his other father notes ("no one can change because it benefits him to believe that"; the "manic / waves" image) on the [[Family and Personal Life]] page, and resonates with the trauma-and-return themes he gravitates to in [[Home (novel)|Home]] and [[It's Not in My Control]].
