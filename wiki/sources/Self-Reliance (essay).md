# Self-Reliance (essay)

Traveler's response to Emerson's [[Self-Reliance]], titled "I do not wish to expiate, but to live."

## Summary
The most personal of his analytical essays: he connects Emerson's anti-conformity to his own **non-religion at an Episcopal school** — rejecting "original sin," sitting out chapel. Nuanced: religion is useful for those lacking "strong foundations based on education and moral standards," but "mindlessly being religious because everyone else is" is the real error. The objection is to conformity, not belief.
