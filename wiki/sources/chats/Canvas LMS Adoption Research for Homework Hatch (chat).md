# Canvas LMS Adoption Research for Homework Hatch

A short ChatGPT (gpt-4o) session from August 2025 that opens as throwaway curiosity — a "disney world parks" query yielding a full Walt Disney World overview (four parks, 2025 expansions like Piston Peak, Villains Land, Monsters Inc. land) — then abruptly pivots into market research that is clearly load-bearing for [[Homework Hatch (startup)]].

## What he was actually doing
The substantive turn is Traveler asking for **a complete list of colleges that use Canvas**, then narrowing to "top 50 colleges by enrollment that use Canvas." This is classic startup TAM/integration scoping: Homework Hatch ingests assignments from school [[learning management system|LMS]] platforms, so knowing which institutions run Canvas (vs. Blackboard, Moodle, Brightspace) maps both the integration target and the addressable market. The Disney detour reads as idle browsing immediately before the real question.

## Key takeaways from the answers
- Canvas (Instructure) is used by **700+ institutions globally**, roughly **half of all North American college students**, including **every Ivy League** and all U.S. News Top-10 universities. Duke migrated off Sakai to Canvas in mid-2025.
- The model produced a ranked list of 50 large-enrollment Canvas schools — Texas A&M, UCF (Webcourses@UCF), Florida, Ohio State (CarmenCanvas), ASU, the entire UC system, Penn State, Rutgers, the CSU system, etc.
- Practical tip surfaced: Instructure's "Find My School" tool lets you confirm a specific institution's LMS — useful for verifying integration targets one school at a time.
