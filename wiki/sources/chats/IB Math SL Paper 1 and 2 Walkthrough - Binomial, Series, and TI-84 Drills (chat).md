# IB Math SL Paper 1 and 2 Walkthrough - Binomial, Series, and TI-84 Drills

The night before his **last [[IB Math (SL)]] exam** (two papers across two days), [[Traveler Stansberry]] fed ChatGPT a 2023 AA SL **Paper 1** (non-calculator) and asked for full step-by-step solutions while he worked it live like "a proper exam-goblin." The model solved Q1-Q9: perpendicular lines, integrating a split surd, box-plot outliers and two-regression-line means, composite trig functions, a product-rule tangent-parallel problem, concavity and inflection from $f''$, geometric/arithmetic series in $\ln x$, and a binomial-theorem-into-trig-identity chain (recognizing $1-3\cos2x+3\cos^2 2x-\cos^3 2x=(1-\cos2x)^3=8\sin^6x$, then $u$-substitution to integrate).

What it reveals is sharp metacognition about his own gaps. After the run he announced: "i got everything right without your help **besides** the arithmetic and geometric series, binomial theorem, and the statistics stuff — which is exactly what i thought would happen." He requested two Word docs: a full IB-formatted review-plus-practice packet with diagrams, and a 10-core-concepts equation sheet for probability, stats, sequences/series, and the binomial theorem — having the model pull from the actual AA SL syllabus and formula booklet. Because he had only just learned the **binomial theorem**, he made the model unpack the full $(x+y)^n=\sum\binom{n}{r}x^{n-r}y^r$ formula term by term (sigma notation, $\binom{n}{r}$ via factorials, Pascal's-triangle coefficients).

For **Paper 2** (calculator section) he openly admitted he wouldn't actually do it — "its pretty easy once i format the equations" — and just wanted answers: compound interest, frequency-table standard deviation, sector segments, discrete random variables, particle motion, independent events, a 3D pyramid, a rational function and its inverse, and a normal-distribution muffin problem. He caught the model's own error (it missed half-yearly compounding on Q1) and then asked for a question-by-question **TI-84 keystroke guide** (1-Var Stats with frequency lists, intersect/zero via graphing, RADIAN mode).
