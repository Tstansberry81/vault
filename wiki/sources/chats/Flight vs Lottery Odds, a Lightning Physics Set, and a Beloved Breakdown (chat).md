## HL Physics — thundercloud electrostatics
A four-part [[IB Physics (HL)]] problem set modeling a thundercloud as a parallel-plate capacitor:
- Surface charge density σ = Q/A = 35 / (1.2×10⁷) = 2.92×10⁻⁶ C/m²
- Field strength E = σ/ε₀ = **3.3×10⁵ V/m**
- Second modeling assumption: cloud base and ground treated as large, effectively-infinite parallel plates (edge/fringing effects neglected) — the IB "model simplification" move
- Discharge time t = Q/I = 35 / 1800 = **0.019 s** (19 ms)
- Energy released E = QV = 35 × 2.5×10⁸ = **8.8×10⁹ J**
