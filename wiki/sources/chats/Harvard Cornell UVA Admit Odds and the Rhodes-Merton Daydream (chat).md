# Harvard, Cornell, UVA Admit Odds and the Rhodes-Merton Daydream

A college-admissions strategy chat from October 2025, mid-senior-year, in which [[Traveler Stansberry]] feeds ChatGPT his full Common App and asks how his profile stacks up against elite schools. It is the clearest single snapshot of his application package and his strategic reasoning behind committing Early Decision to [[UVA and the Quant Question|UVA]].

## The profile, as it stood Oct 2025
The chat confirms and consolidates many details scattered across the wiki:
- **Academics:** 4.0 unweighted GPA; full [[IB Diploma|IB Diploma]] candidate with HL [[IB Physics (HL)|Physics]], HL [[English Curriculum 2022-2026|English]], HL [[IB History (HL)|History]], SL [[IB Math (SL)|Math AA]], SL [[IB Economics (SL)|Economics]], SL [[IB Japanese (SL)|Japanese]].
- **Testing:** SAT 1540 (760 R/W, 780 M), up from an earlier sitting; ACT 33.
- **Honors:** Cum Laude Society, National Merit Commended, Japanese Honors, IB Diploma Candidate.
- **Extracurriculars:** [[Homework Hatch (startup)|Homework Hatch]] (co-founder — flagged repeatedly as his "crown jewel" / "spike"), [[Investment Club]] (president), Bridges volunteer counselor (named Volunteer of the Year), Civic Engagement Club co-president, NSLC Business Program, the [[Extended Essay (Economics)|Economics EE]] on fiscal policy and inflation, choir tour in Greece, JV squash co-captain, and the Japanese study trip to Rikkyo Niiza.
- **Personal essay:** the "getting lost in Tokyo / learning adaptability" narrative (see [[Japan Study Abroad Common App Essay - Getting Lost and Adapting (chat)]]).

The model's read: academics "Harvard-level strong," ECs "distinct and self-driven" with a coherent builder/entrepreneur identity, but the personal essay "strong concept, moderate execution" — too conventional in its "challenge → growth → belonging" arc to be a committee-stopper.

## The strategic reasoning
Traveler is explicit that he is applying **ED to UVA**, and presses the model for the cold math. The takeaway he confirms: ED UVA (~20-25% OOS) beats Harvard restrictive EA (~6-8%, no real boost) or Cornell ED (Dyson ~8-10%, "basically UVA RD odds in disguise"). The model frames UVA ED as "maximizing probability... choosing sanity over status."

Crucially, his motive isn't purely tactical — he adds "i also just like uva a lot lmao. ivy is kinda dushy [sic]." This matches his documented preference (see [[UVA and the Quant Question]], [[College Search]]) for McIntire-track finance with livability over Ivy prestige, consistent with his [[Political and Economic Views|individualist]] streak and disdain for status-chasing. Intended path: Commerce/Economics, [[The Moral Impact of Slavery|McIntire]]-aligned analytical + leadership combo.

## The Rhodes / Merton tangent
The chat drifts into postgraduate aspiration: Rhodes Scholarship criteria (academic excellence, real leadership, character, service) and **Merton College, Oxford**, which he visited while in London weeks earlier. He notes C.S. Lewis was a fellow there (English tutor, 1925-1954). This is a small but telling window into his [[Intellectual Interests|intellectual ambition]] — he is already idly modeling a path beyond undergrad finance toward Oxford and elite scholarship.

## Why it matters
This is the canonical inventory of his college application as submitted, and documents the rationale behind the ED-UVA decision that defines his college outcome. It also reinforces how central [[Homework Hatch (startup)|Homework Hatch]] is to his self-presentation — across all three schools the model identifies it as the single differentiator that lifts him from "qualified" to "distinctive."
