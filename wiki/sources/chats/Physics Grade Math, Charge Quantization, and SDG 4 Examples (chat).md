## Physics concepts
A run of [[IB Physics (HL)]] review topics, all in his electromagnetism unit:
- **Quantization of charge** — Robert Millikan's 1909 oil-drop experiment showed charge comes in integer multiples of the elementary charge (q = n·e); J.J. Thomson discovered the electron earlier, but Millikan measured and quantized its charge.
- **Faraday's and Lenz's laws** — induced emf from changing magnetic flux (ℰ = −dΦ/dt), with Lenz's law supplying the direction (opposing the change) and explaining the negative sign as conservation of energy in disguise.
- **Electric fields and charge motion** — field points + → −, positive charges accelerate with the field, negatives against; uniform fields (parallel plates) give constant acceleration, and sideways entry yields parabolic projectile-style motion.

These map to the same HL electricity/induction material seen in his other physics chats (e.g. permittivity, electron-in-a-field, and magnetism drills).
