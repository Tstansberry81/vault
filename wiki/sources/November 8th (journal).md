# November 8th (journal)

A short journal entry (titled for the night of Dom's 18th birthday).

## Summary
Written "slightly intoxicated… in Cole's car," again noting the **numbness** ("I keep trying to force myself to feel things"). The week's blow: **getting cut from the Varsity Squash team senior year** — which he ties to a lifelong intolerance of **failure and inadequacy** ("Failure to me has always been unacceptable… makes me feel unworthy"). Pushes back on his dad's saying "there's no such thing as 'can't'" ("the older I get the more bullshit I think that is"), and admits his academic success came "because I was smart… not because I tried hard."

## Why it matters
A perfectionism/failure counterpoint to his composure elsewhere, and a corrective to the [[Extracurriculars and Achievements|résumé]] (which lists squash through senior co-captaincy — but he was *cut* from Varsity as a senior). Part of [[Emotional Life and Inner World]].
