# Major Moments Memoir

Personal memoir ([[English Curriculum 2022-2026|English 9]]), subtitled "Sons, You Are Going to Have a Brother."

## Summary
At 14, Traveler learns his father — between girlfriends — fathered a child, then started dating Shannon (whom he'd marry). The memoir moves through a family trip to Mexico (and food poisoning in Sayulita) into a "wave of emotion": anger at his dad and even at the unborn child, guilt for that anger, and finally **kinship** when he meets his baby brother **Reece** ("not feeling indifference, but feeling kinship"). Rich sensory, image-driven prose.

## Why it matters
A foundational document of his family life — the half-brother, the father's remarriage — and an early example of his strength with imagery and emotional honesty. Feeds [[Traveler Stansberry]].
