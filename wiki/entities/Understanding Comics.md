# Understanding Comics

Nonfiction (in comics form) by **Scott McCloud**. Studied in [[English Curriculum 2022-2026|English 9]], paired with [[Maus]] as a lens on the medium.

## Traveler's reading ([[Understanding Comics Ch1]])
McCloud's dilemma: he loves comics but is mocked for it. The key definition Traveler extracts: comics are "juxtaposed pictorial and other images in a deliberate sequence, intended to convey information and/or to produce an aesthetic response." Notes McCloud's "genius" in using comics to explain comics, and that the medium can be "more memorable than reading words on a page." Ancient precedents: pre-Columbians, Egyptians, Normans.
