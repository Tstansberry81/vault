# The Other Wes Moore

Memoir by **Wes Moore** (*One Name, Two Fates* — two men, same name, opposite outcomes). Studied in [[English Curriculum 2022-2026|English 9]] (Mr. Bryant, fall 2022).

## Traveler's readings
- **What separated their fates** ([[Wes Paragraph1]]): "the help from others" — supportive family, private school, the grandparents who mortgaged their home for military academy. Both Weses were tempted by drug money; only one had the resources to resist.
- **Choice & responsibility** ([[Mid Term Exam Essay]]): pairs the Other Wes's relapse into dealing with [[A Sound of Thunder|Eckels]] stepping off the path — "they have no one to blame but themselves." Agency over circumstance.
- **Personal connection** ([[Which Wes Moore I relate to the most (at this point)]]): relates via the **private-school experience** as an "escape" — directly tying the text to his own life at [[St. Paul's School]].
