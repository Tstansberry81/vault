# Porter Stansberry (father)

**Frank Porter Stansberry** (b. 1972), who goes by **Porter** — [[Traveler Stansberry]]'s father, and a well-known figure in financial publishing: founder of **Stansberry Research** and, more recently, **Porter & Co.** (the *Porter & Co. Daily Journal*). The "new business" Traveler references in his 2022 [[Letter to Dad (Dec 2022)|letter]] is Porter & Co.

## How the wiki connects him
The identification reconciles two names cleanly: the [[Common App (UVA ED 2026)]] lists Traveler's father as "Frank Stansberry," while his ChatGPT chats show his dad forwarding *Porter & Co.* newsletters ("Fellas — give this one a read") — same person, professional name "Porter." It's further confirmed by **Shannon Stansberry**, named in the newsletter as Porter's wife and in the Common App as Traveler's stepmother (see [[Family and Personal Life]]).

## The relationship
Documented at length under [[Family and Personal Life]] and [[Emotional Life and Inner World]]: a fraught, central, darkening thread. Traveler describes him as **hyper-intelligent but low-empathy** (he uses the word *narcissist*), prone to "manic" spending "waves," an atheist who nonetheless "believes in objective morality," and the driver in a frightening Halloween drunk-driving incident. His deepest fear is inheriting these traits — "I see some of him in myself."

## Intellectual influence — and independence
His father's finance-libertarian world is plainly the soil for Traveler's own [[Investment Club|markets vocation]], [[Political and Economic Views|free-market views]], and [[Quantitative Finance|quant ambitions]]. But the chats show Traveler engaging it **critically, not absorbing it**: he summarized *and dismantled* a Porter & Co. essay (by Shannon Stansberry) on "gender specialization," separating its valid Econ-101 core from its "ideological leap" — a sharp instance of reasoning against his own father's milieu ([[Porter Stansberry Newsletter on Gender Specialization, Pilots, and IQ (chat)]]).

> [!update] Traveler is building for his father's brand (2026-06-18)
> The June 2026 code ingest makes the influence concrete and *forward-looking*, not just inherited: Traveler's quant model now ships a consumer front-end, **[[Vision (Porter Intelligence)]]**, deliberately styled in the **"Porter Intelligence"** house look, and the [[The Edge (trading model)|Edge]] engine's code docstring brands the trading product for **"Porter & Co."** So he isn't only reasoning *against* his father's milieu (as in the gender-specialization teardown below) — he's also building a product *for* that world. The critical-engagement and the apprenticeship run in parallel.
