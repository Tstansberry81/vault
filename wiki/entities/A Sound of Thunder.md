# A Sound of Thunder

Short story by **Ray Bradbury** (time-travel dinosaur safari; one crushed butterfly rewrites history). Studied in [[English Curriculum 2022-2026|English 9]] (Mr. Bryant, Sept 2022) — the subject of his first major character analysis.

## Traveler's readings
- **[[Character Analysis Essay|"A Study of Eckels: The Arrogant Thrill Seeker"]]**: Eckels is "ignorant, arrogant, and fearful"; his refusal to heed others drives the disaster. Thesis: "Arrogance can blind someone from the truth." His first formal MLA character essay.
- **Consequence & agency** ([[Mid Term Exam Essay]]): Eckels stepping off the path = choices having unforeseen consequences; paired with [[The Other Wes Moore]] on personal responsibility. An early seed of [[Fate and Free Will|determinism/consequence]].

## Themes
[[Fate and Free Will|consequence & determinism]] · arrogance · person vs. nature
