# The Pearl

Novella by **John Steinbeck**. Referenced (not formally studied in an essay) as the explicit model for Traveler's original short story [[The One]].

## Role in the corpus
Traveler uses *The Pearl*'s Kino — destroyed by a market and society arrayed against an individual's aspiration — as the template for his anti-hustle-culture parable. Quotes he wove in as chapter epigraphs:
- "humans are never satisfied… one of the greatest talents the species has"
- "The pearl has become my soul. If I give it up I shall lose my soul"
- "it would be a shame if the pearl destroyed him"
