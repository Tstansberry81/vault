# n8n (automation platform)

The visual workflow-automation tool [[Traveler Stansberry]] adopted to build the automation layer of [[Homework Hatch (startup)]] (and which appears on his résumé skills list).

## How he used it
He built an "AI secretary" — branded **"Anna"** / the HW Buddy assistant — in n8n: a **Telegram voice note → Google Calendar** pipeline (plagued by a chronic date-drift bug he debugged at length), a **command router** spanning Telegram, Google Calendar, and Instagram, and Instagram marketing automation. He also used it to *learn* the brain–memory–tools structure of AI agents ([[AI Agents in n8n Explained - Brain-Memory-Tools and the HW Buddy Blueprint (chat)]]).
