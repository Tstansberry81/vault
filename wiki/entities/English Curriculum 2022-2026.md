# English Curriculum 2022–2026

The four-year arc behind this wiki, reconstructed from dated assignments by [[Traveler Stansberry]] at [[St. Paul's School]].

## English 9 — Mr. Bryant, G Block (2022–2023)
Short stories and first novels; foundational skills (MLA, thesis, character analysis).
- Short stories: [[A Sound of Thunder]], [[The Lottery]], [[A&P]]
- Novels/graphic novels: [[Maus]] (Part I), [[Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep?]], [[The Other Wes Moore]], [[Understanding Comics]]
- Drama: [[Romeo and Juliet]]
- Personal writing: [[Life Map]], [[Major Moments Memoir]], [[Steamboat Times]], [[When I Feel Like an Outsider]]

## English 10 — Mr. Bryant (2023–2024)
American literature, social criticism, transcendentalism.
- [[Narrative of the Life of Frederick Douglass]] · [[The Great Gatsby]] · [[One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest]] · [[Station Eleven]] · [[Home (novel)|Home]] (Toni Morrison)
- Transcendentalism: [[Self-Reliance]] & [[Civil Disobedience]] (Emerson/Thoreau)

## IB HL English 1 — Dr. Harris (2024–2025)
Classical and dramatic texts; the IB **Individual Oral (IO)**.
- [[The Iliad]] · [[A Streetcar Named Desire]]
- IO texts: [[Antigone]], [[The Lion in Winter]], [[Absalom, Absalom!]] — see [[Individual Oral (IO)]]

> Senior-year full IB load (from his [[Academic Record|report card]]): HL English/Physics/History, SL Math (AA)/Economics/Japanese, + Theory of Knowledge. See [[Academic Record]].

## IB HL English 2 — Mr. Mezeske (2025–2026)
Identity-focused unit; the IB **Paper 2** comparative essay.
- [[Beloved]] · [[The Narrow Road to the Deep North]] · [[The Elephant Vanishes]] (Murakami) · [[Garden of Eden (poem)|Garden of Eden]] & "Wade in the Water" (Tracy K. Smith) · [[The Mower (poem)|The Mower]] (Larkin) · [[Death of a Salesman]]
- Original creative work: [[The One]] (modeled on Steinbeck's [[The Pearl]])
- See [[Paper 2 Study Guide]]

## Trajectory
Foundational analysis → American social criticism → classical/IB rigor → identity & comparative synthesis. The recurring themes ([[Fate and Free Will]], [[Identity and Its Collapse]], [[Individual vs. Society]]) are seeded early and deepen each year.
