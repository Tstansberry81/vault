# Individual Oral (IO)

The IB **Individual Oral** assessment Traveler prepared in [[English Curriculum 2022-2026|HL English 1]]: a ~5-minute recorded oral analyzing a **global issue** across one literary and one non-literary/translated work, supported by two ≤30-line extracts and a ≤10-bullet outline ([[IO Notes Guideline]]).

## His topic
**Field of inquiry: Politics, Power, and Justice** → narrowed to *hierarchies of power / patriarchy*.

## How it evolved (a process worth preserving)
1. **[[Global Issue Activity|First brainstorm]]:** [[Antigone]] + [[Absalom, Absalom!]] — "the search for power… ultimately causes [Creon and Sutpen] to be ruined by their own creations."
2. **[[IO Outline and Extracts|Mid draft]]:** switched the pairing to [[Antigone]] + [[The Lion in Winter]], theme of male dominance vs. capable women.
3. **[[Outline IB Form|Final outline]]:** thesis on patriarchy as a tool to uphold hierarchy; Creon and Henry isolate Antigone and Eleanor for rebelling.
