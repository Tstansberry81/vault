# Investment Club

[[Traveler Stansberry]] is **President** of his school's Investment Club — the clearest expression of his [[College Search|finance vocation]] outside the classroom.

## What he built
A genuinely ambitious **45-week curriculum** he authored, progressing from fundamentals to advanced valuation:
- Basics: markets, asset classes (stocks/bonds/ETFs), risk vs. reward, diversification
- Valuation & analysis: market cap, P/E, EPS, **ROIC, DuPont/ROE**, P/B, FCF, PEG, D/E, reading 10-Ks/10-Qs, moats
- Advanced: **DCF & comparables**, beta/volatility, technical vs. fundamental analysis, options (calls/puts), short selling/margin, behavioral finance, **mock-portfolio competitions**

## The investing he actually does (ChatGPT record, ~22 finance chats)
The export shows the club curriculum is no abstraction — he runs the same playbook on real companies, and teaches himself the tools:
- **Self-directed equity research:** a sustained specialty-insurance thesis on **Arch Capital** and **Palomar**, plus an assembly-line **qualitative moat/market-share screen across ~20 companies** ([[Insurance Equity Research - Arch Capital and Palomar (chat)]], [[Qualitative Stock Screening - Moats and Market Share Across 20 Companies (chat)]]).
- **[[Bloomberg Terminal]] fluency:** EQS screening, the exact Excel add-in formulas (`BDP`/`BDH`), and pulling ROIC/EPS/beta ([[Bloomberg EQS Screening and Insurance Stock Research (chat)]], [[Bloomberg Excel Functions - YoY Revenue, EPS, and Beta (chat)]]).
- **His own tools & paper:** a **[[Personal Quant Model]]** he designed (multi-factor scoring, HMM regimes, Carhart attribution — his methodology, AI-coded to spec), which by mid-2026 he split into a product stack — **[[The Edge (trading model)]]** (a short-horizon momentum/acceleration trader) feeding **[[Vision (Porter Intelligence)]]** (a consumer site under his father's [[Porter Stansberry (father)|Porter & Co.]] brand); the earlier AI **stock-ranking app** ("[[Sauron Investing]]"); and an original investing working paper — the **[[Honeycomb Portfolio]]** (kissing-number geometry + a survivorship-bias self-critique).

## Read for the [[UVA and the Quant Question|quant question]] — and the pivot
This started as **fundamental/value investing** (Buffett-flavored — DCF, moats, management quality), strong evidence he's a natural **finance** person. But the late-senior chats show him deliberately pushing toward the quant lane: the no-arbitrage principle, what a derivative is, the Black-Scholes derivation, "the basic assumptions quants make," and a deep dive on **Renaissance Technologies / the Medallion Fund**. The center of gravity is still markets-finance, but he's actively building toward [[Quantitative Finance]] — see [[UVA and the Quant Question]].
